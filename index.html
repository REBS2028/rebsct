Every Action Counts. Together We Make a Difference.

If you’re waking up week after week dreading the barrage of daily horrors coming from the American government, then you — like us — are probably wondering what you can do to help restore democracy to our beloved country. It is our belief that We the People ARE the United States. It's not the people in the White House, on Capitol Hill, or any other federal building.

We have a process whereby we elect representatives to bring our ideas and viewpoints to Congress. As such, We the People are in charge of the United States and it’s our responsibility to commit ourselves to see that this privilege does not get obliterated by the current administration.

So, let’s get busy TOGETHER!
